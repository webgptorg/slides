# Promptbook — Investor Pitch Deck

_Last updated: 2026-03-20_

---

## 1) Vision

Build enterprise AI agents that are a true extension of a company's expertise and values — reliable, auditable, and portable.

Source: Promptbook product & mission

---

## 2) Problem

- Enterprises struggle to turn scattered company knowledge (policies, docs, tacit expertise) into predictable, auditable AI behaviors.
- Prompt drift, unpredictable model outputs, and weak enforcement of rules make AI unsafe for high-stakes business use.

Evidence and product positioning: Promptbook addresses context, rules, and persona explicitly to solve these problems

---

## 3) Solution — Promptbook

- Book language: a human-friendly DSL to define Persona, Knowledge, Rule, Action commitments that become enforceable AI behavior.
- Promptbook Engine: runs Books, manages RAG, enforces rules (adversary checks), and controls model parameters.
- Deployable Agents: Servers and SDKs to run agents in chat, email, coding assistants, and internal apps.

Product details and ecosystem: ptbk.io and project repo
