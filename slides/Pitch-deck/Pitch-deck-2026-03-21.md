# Promptbook

## Ship reliable, controllable AI — write your company as code

### Pavol Hejný (CTO) & Jiří Jahn (CEO)

### 2026-03-21

---

# The problem

- Enterprises struggle to turn scattered knowledge, policies and processes into reliable AI: pilots don't scale, compliance is hard, and control is lost.
- No-code tools trade off control for simplicity; engineering-heavy approaches are slow and brittle.

---

# Our solution — Book + Promptbook

- Book: a human-friendly language to describe Personas, Knowledge, Rules and Actions — executable "Books" that define AI agents.
- Promptbook Engine & Server: runtime, tooling and deployment for Books — enabling reproducible, auditable and portable AI agents.

(Models are interchangeable — Promptbook focuses on behavior, rules and knowledge.)

---

# Key benefits

- Business people author AI in plain language; devs ship to production.
- Predictable behavior via explicit Rule commitments and audit logs.
- Portable blueprints that run across environments (cloud, on-prem).

---

# Product highlights (what's new / notable)

- Open-source monorepo with modular packages (engine, server, integrations).
- Integrations for major LLMs and vendors — including Gemini support in recent releases.
- Tools for document ingestion (PDF, DOCX), RAG management, and JS execution inside Books.

---

# Pricing (public)

- Free: Starter tier — 1 agent, basic knowledge and community support.
- Pro: $499/year — up to 10 agents, advanced rules, action commitments, priority support.
- Enterprise: custom — unlimited agents, on-prem, SLA, dedicated support.

(See public pricing pages for full details and municipal offers.)

---

# Go-to-market

- Direct sales to mid-market and enterprises; vertical plays (municipalities / government) with tailored packages and integration services.
- Developer-first community and open-source distribution to drive adoption and templates.
- Partner channels for systems integrators and local vendors.

---

# Municipal vertical (example)

- Tailored AI agents for cities: virtual experts for citizens and clerks, automated email processing, document compliance checks.
- Local pricing tiers for municipalities with packages scaled by document volume and number of agents.

---

# Traction & ecosystem

- Active open-source project with packaged NPM modules and Docker images.
- Community on Discord and GitHub discussions; templates and miniapps for rapid demos.

---

# Roadmap

- Short term: improved model adapters, enhanced RAG tuning, enterprise connectors.
- Mid term: certifications (SOC2/ISO), expanded analytics and governance tooling.
- Long term: deeper multimodal workflows and marketplace for Books and templates.

---

# Security & governance

- On-prem deployment option, end-to-end encryption, granular access controls and audit logging.
- Versioning of Books for traceability and compliance workflows.
