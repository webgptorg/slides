# Levels of abstraction

**Three-tier architecture for enterprise AI**

1. **Applications Layer**: User-facing AI applications and interfaces

    - Custom chatbots, document processors, decision support systems
    - Business-specific workflows and user experiences

2. **Orchestration Layer (Promptbook)**: The intelligent middleware

    - Workflow management, persona handling, context management
    - Quality assurance, compliance enforcement, audit trails

3. **Models Layer**: Foundation AI models and services
    - OpenAI, Anthropic, Azure OpenAI, custom models
    - Embedding services, specialized AI tools

# RAG, MCP and function calling

**Advanced AI capabilities for enterprise workflows**

**Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)**

-   Connect AI to your enterprise knowledge base
-   Real-time access to documents, databases, and internal systems
-   Contextually relevant responses based on your organization's data

**Model Context Protocol (MCP)**

-   Standardized way to connect AI models with external tools and data sources
-   Secure, scalable integration with enterprise systems
-   Future-proof architecture for evolving AI landscape

**Function Calling**

-   AI can execute specific business functions and API calls
-   Automated workflow execution and system integration
-   Safe, controlled AI actions with proper authorization
