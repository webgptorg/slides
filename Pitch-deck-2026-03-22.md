# Promptbook — AI Agents as Company Knowledge

Jiří Jahn (CEO) • Pavol Hejný (CTO)
Investor Pitch Deck — 2026-03-22

---

## Problem

- Companies struggle to turn scattered documents, rules and tribal knowledge into reliable AI behavior.
- Prompt drift, inconsistent personalities, and hidden model parameters make AI unsafe for business processes.
- No simple, auditable way for non-engineers to define rules, knowledge and behavior for production AI agents.

---

## Solution

Promptbook: a Book language + Engine for defining, testing and deploying AI agents that faithfully encode a company's knowledge, rules and personality.
- Human-friendly "Book" language for Persona, Knowledge, Rules and Actions.
- Engine that enforces commitments, manages RAG, and runs agents across channels.
- Portable, auditable, and deployable (cloud or on-prem).

---

## Product Highlights

- Book language: high-level, commitment-based syntax for precise agent behavior.
- Promptbook Engine: runs Books, converts commitments to model params, RAG, adversary checks and enforcement.
- Integrations: OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, Azure, Ollama, Vercel and vector stores.

---

## Market & Use Cases

- Internal assistants (HR, Legal, Ops)
- Customer support & reply agents
- Municipal / Govtech deployments (local government knowledge & citizen assistants)
- Developer tooling: coding agents enforcing style & architecture
